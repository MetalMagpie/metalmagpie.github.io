Money Management

We work with a wide range of individuals and groups to increase financial capability.

"A financially capable person is someone who keeps track of their money, plans ahead and is able to make informed decisions about their finances, understanding the risks and benefits of particular options."

~ Money Advice Service

Frances Burton has over 15 years experience of designing and delivering money management programmes. She also trains and supports others in the design and delivery of such programmes and helps front-line staff, such as debt and advice workers, housing officers, youth workers and teachers, integrate money management into their work.

Topics covered include:

  • Budgeting
  • Borrowing and saving
  • Dealing with debt
  • Opening and managing bank accounts
  • The psychology of money
