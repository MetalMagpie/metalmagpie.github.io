Training & Development

Utilising over 30 years of experience, Frances Burton works with private sector, public sector and voluntary organisations to identify and meet their learning and development needs and achieve their goals.

The areas in which we help organisations to increase their capability include:

  • developing effective managers and leaders
  • identifying and meeting client/customer needs
  • business planning
  • strategic planning
  • leading high performing teams
  • performance management - supervision and appraisal
  • dealing with difficult clients/customers
  • managing in a multi-cultural environment

Key skills that we help managers and workers to develop include:

  • communication
  • negotiation
  • team working
  • coaching
  • delegation
  • presenting
  • time management
  • change management

The Way We Work

We agree with clients a bespoke development programme designed to achieve specific performance objectives. The programme may be tailored to address a particular challenge, support and develop strengths or facilitate individual or organisational development.

Our development programmes can include 1-1 coaching or mentoring, facilitation of any size group (from a small team to a whole organisational review) and in house training courses (half a day, 1-2 days or a number of shorter linked sessions).

We create a supportive, empowering and interactive learning environment, in which participants can develop the skills and confidence they need to maximise their own and their organisation's potential.
